About us

Agumentik Educamp

Agumentik Educamp Pvt. Ltd., a part of the Agumentik Group of companies, is a registered company with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) of the Government of India since 2021. The company was established with the aim of assisting interested candidates in becoming experts in demanding skill sets.

The company's expert team emphasizes practical education over theoretical concepts, as practical application allows individuals to grasp concepts more efficiently and enables them to explain those concepts in their own words.
