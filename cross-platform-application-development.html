Overview

The Cross Development course offered by Agumentik Educamp covers a range of technologies and frameworks, including Figma, Dart, Flutter, GetX, Node.js, Laravel, and React Native. This course aims to provide participants with the skills necessary to develop cross-platform mobile applications.

What You will Learn?

  • Introduction to Mobile App Development and Design Principles
  • Dart and Flutter Fundamentals for Cross-Platform Development
  • Flutter App Development with GetX State Management
  • Cross-Platform Development with React Native
  • Backend Development with Node.js and Laravel
  • Advanced Concepts and Project Development
