Overview

The HR Training course offered by Agumentik Educamp covers a wide range of topics related to human resources management, including strategies, recruitment planning, talent acquisition, team management, payroll, compliance, performance review, university and corporate relations, and day-to-day HR responsibilities. This course aims to provide participants with the knowledge and skills required for a successful career in HR.

What You will Learn?

  • Overview of HR: Understand HR roles, strategic importance, and modern workplace challenges.
  • Recruitment and Talent Acquisition: Learn strategies for sourcing, interviewing, onboarding, and orientation.
  • Team Management: Master team dynamics, employee engagement, conflict resolution, and retention.
  • Payroll and Compensation: Explore payroll processes, compensation administration, and incentive programs.
  • HR Compliance: Comply with employment laws, diversity and inclusion, and ethical HR practices.
  • Performance Management: Implement performance reviews, goal setting, coaching, and appraisal systems.
