Overview

The Digital Marketing Training course offered by Agumentik Educamp covers a comprehensive range of topics in digital marketing, including marketing strategies, lead generation, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing (SMM), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, content writing, poster designing, email marketing, WhatsApp marketing, effective communication, client handling, bidding platforms for lead generation, customer success, market research, report writing, presentation skills, university and corporate relations, and day-to-day digital marketing responsibilities.

What You will Learn?

  • Introduction to Digital Marketing and Marketing Strategies.
  • Lead Generation and Conversion Optimization.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Techniques.
  • Social Media Marketing (SMM) and Advertising.
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising and Campaign Management.
  • Content Writing, Poster Designing, and Visual Assets Creation.
  • Weeks 1-2

    Introduction to Digital Marketing and Marketing Strategies

    • Lecture 1: Overview of digital marketing channels and techniques
    • Lecture 2: Understanding marketing objectives and target audience
    • Lecture 3: Developing effective digital marketing strategies
  • Weeks 3-4

    Lead Generation and Conversion Optimization

    • Lecture 1: Lead generation techniques and strategies
    • Lecture 2: Landing page optimization and conversion rate optimization
    • Lecture 3: Creating effective call-to-action (CTA) and lead capture forms
  • Weeks 5-6

    Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

    • Lecture 1: Understanding SEO principles and techniques
    • Lecture 2: Keyword research and on-page optimization
    • Lecture 3: Off-page optimization and link building strategies
    • Lecture 4: Monitoring and reporting SEO performance
  • Weeks 7-8

    Social Media Marketing (SMM)

    • Lecture 1: Introduction to social media platforms and their marketing potential
    • Lecture 2: Creating engaging social media content and campaigns
    • Lecture 3: Social media advertising and audience targeting
    • Lecture 4: Social media analytics and performance tracking
  • Weeks 9-10

    Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

    • Lecture 1: Introduction to PPC advertising platforms (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.)
    • Lecture 2: Creating effective ad campaigns and ad copies
    • Lecture 3: Keyword targeting, bidding strategies, and budget management
    • Lecture 4: Monitoring and optimizing PPC campaigns
  • Weeks 11-12

    Content Writing and Poster Designing

    • Lecture 1: Content writing techniques for digital marketing
    • Lecture 2: Creating compelling and SEO-friendly content
    • Lecture 3: Designing attractive posters and visual assets
    • Lecture 4: Tools and software for content creation and design
  • Weeks 13-14

    Email Marketing and WhatsApp Marketing

    • Lecture 1: Developing email marketing campaigns and automation
    • Lecture 2: Writing persuasive email copies and subject lines
    • Lecture 3: Leveraging WhatsApp for marketing and customer engagement
    • Lecture 4: Compliance with email marketing and messaging regulations
  • Weeks 15-16

    Effective Communication and Client Handling

    • Lecture 1: Developing strong communication skills for client interaction
    • Lecture 2: Active listening and effective questioning techniques
    • Lecture 3: Building rapport with clients and understanding their needs
    • Lecture 3: Handling client requests, feedback, and objections
  • Weeks 17-18

    Market Research and Report Writing

    • Lecture 1: Conducting market research and competitor analysis
    • Lecture 2: Analyzing consumer behavior and market trends
    • Lecture 3: Creating comprehensive marketing reports and insights
    • Lecture 4: Data visualization and reporting tools
  • Weeks 19-20

    Presentation Skills and Internship Preparation

    • Lecture 1: Developing effective presentation skills for marketing pitches
    • Lecture 2: Preparing internship objectives and deliverables
    • Lecture 3: Developing a professional portfolio and resume
  • Weeks 21-22

    Internship (2 months)

    • Lecture 1: Applying digital marketing knowledge and skills in real-world scenarios
    • Lecture 2: Assisting with digital marketing projects and campaigns
    • Lecture 3: Gaining practical experience in various digital marketing functions
