Overview

The Sales Training course offered by Agumentik Educamp focuses on developing essential sales skills and knowledge to succeed in a competitive business environment. The course covers a wide range of topics, including sales strategies, lead generation, email marketing, WhatsApp marketing, effective communication, client handling, bidding platforms for lead generation, customer success, market research, report writing, presentation skills, university and corporate relations, and day-to-day sales responsibilities.

What You will Learn?

  • Introduction to Sales and Sales Strategies
  • Lead Generation and Prospecting Techniques
  • Email Marketing and WhatsApp Marketing for Sales
  • Effective Communication and Client Handling
  • Customer Success and Relationship Management
  • Market Research and Competitive Analysis

    • Lecture 1: Overview of sales roles and responsibilities
    • Lecture 2: Sales process and pipeline management
    • Lecture 3: Developing effective sales strategies
  • Weeks 3-4

    Lead Generation and Prospecting

    • Lecture 1: Lead generation techniques and strategies

    • Lecture 3: Qualifying leads and prospecting methods
    • Lecture 4: Using bidding platforms for lead generation
  • Weeks 5-6

    Email Marketing and WhatsApp Marketing

    • Lecture 1: Creating effective email marketing campaigns
    • Lecture 2: Writing compelling sales emails
    • Lecture 3: Using automation tools for email marketing
    • Lecture 4: Leveraging WhatsApp for sales and customer engagement
  • Weeks 7-8

    Effective Communication and Client Handling

    • Lecture 1: Developing strong communication skills
    • Lecture 2: Active listening and effective questioning techniques
    • Lecture 3: Building rapport with clients
    • Lecture 4: Handling objections and closing deals
  • Weeks 9-10

    Customer Success and Relationship Management

    • Lecture 1: Understanding customer success principles

    Market Research and Competitive Analysis

    • Lecture 1: Conducting market research and analysis

    Sales Reporting and Presentation Skills

    University and Corporate Relations

  • Weeks 17-18

    Day-to-Day Sales Operations

    • Lecture 1: Managing sales pipelines and CRM systems
    • Lecture 2: Territory management and sales planning

    • Lecture 4: Sales administration and documentation

    Project Development and Internship Preparation
